USE CASES

One platform, endless possibilities

Khayaal isn't just for education. It's a creative AI platform for anyone who wants to bring drawings to life.

Educational Content

Personalized lessons where children learn from their own characters. Math, science, languages — taught by their imagination.

Arabic Storytelling

Bring Arabic folktales and cultural stories to life with custom animated characters. Preserving heritage through creativity.

Content Creation

Creators and storytellers can produce animated videos without any animation skills. From idea to video in minutes.

Brand & Marketing

Businesses can create speaking mascots and branded characters from simple sketches. Stand out with animated content.
